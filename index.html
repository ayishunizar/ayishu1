Project


Automatic Medicine Recommendation System



Nowadays people are progressively started caring about the health and medical diagnosis problems. However, according to the administration’s report, more than one crore people every year die due to medication error done by novices (Newdoctor’s). More than 42% medication errors are caused by doctors because they provide prescriptions according to their experience which are quite limited. And sometimes they have left many parts of the book they have read Technologies as machine learning and recommender technologies provide possibilities to explore potential knowledge from diagnosis history and help the doctors to prescribe medication correctly to decrease the medication error, there by improving the patients health. In this project we are proposing an medicine recommendation system by combining both IOT and machine learning Technologies together, ie through sensors we monitor patient data and this data will feedback to ML module which is already trained with disease and corresponding solution medicines. So this system can produce accurate results by predicting correct medicine for corresponding illness.
